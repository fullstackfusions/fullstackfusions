## The Trail as a System

Every hiking trail is a distributed system:
- **Waypoints** are like microservice endpoints
- **Switchbacks** are retry logic with backoff
- **Summit** is the production deploy

## Lessons

1. **Start early** — just like deployments, mornings are safest
2. **Pack light** — over-engineering slows you down
3. **Know your turnaround time** — deadlines exist for a reason
4. **The view from the top is worth the climb** — shipping feels good
